## AI Processing & Third-Party Services

### Third-Party AI Providers

**LuxScaler uses third-party artificial intelligence services** to process your images. This is core to how the service works.

**When you upload an image:**

1. **Your image is sent to third-party AI providers** via API
2. **Processing occurs on their servers** (not our servers)
3. **AI analyzes and generates new images** based on your input
4. **Results are returned to us** and stored in our system
5. **AI providers may log requests temporarily** for their security and operational purposes

**What data leaves our servers:**
- ‚úì Your uploaded images (full resolution or compressed)
- ‚úì Image metadata (dimensions, format)
- ‚úì Processing settings
- ‚úì System prompts (our instructions to AI)
- ‚úó NOT your account info (email, name, payment data)
- ‚úó NOT your IP address or personal identifiers

**What AI providers may retain:**
- Processing logs (typically 30-90 days)
- Error logs and diagnostics
- Security and abuse detection data
- Exact retention periods are controlled by third parties (outside our control)
