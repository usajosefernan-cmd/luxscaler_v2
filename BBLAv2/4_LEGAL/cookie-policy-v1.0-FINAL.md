## What Are Cookies?

**Cookies are small data files** that websites store on your device (computer, phone, tablet).

**They contain:**
- Unique identifier
- Domain that set it
- Expiration date
- Some data

**Why websites use them:**
- Keep you logged in
- Remember preferences
- Analyze usage
- Prevent fraud

**Types:**
- **Session cookies:** Deleted when you close browser
- **Persistent cookies:** Stored for days/months/years
- **First-party:** Set by LuxScaler
- **Third-party:** Set by other companies

## Cookies We Use

### 1. Essential Cookies (Required)

**Status:** Mandatory - No opt-out option

**Purpose:** Core functionality without which the service can't work

**Cookies:**

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Expires | Provider |
|-------------|---------|---------|----------|
| `session_id` | Keep you logged in | Session or 30 days | LuxScaler |
| `csrf_token` | Prevent cross-site attacks | Session | LuxScaler |
| `auth_token` | Authentication token | 30 days | Supabase Auth |
| `user_id` | Identify logged-in user | Session | LuxScaler |
| `theme_preference` | Remember dark/light mode | 12 months | LuxScaler |

**Cannot be disabled:** Essential cookies are required for the Service to work. If you disable them, you won't be able to:
- Create an account
- Log in
- Generate images
- Access your account

**These are safe:** No personal information is stored in essential cookies. They're just identifiers.

### 2. Analytics Cookies (Optional)

**Status:** Optional - You can opt-out anytime

**Purpose:** Understand how users interact with LuxScaler to improve the service

**Cookies:**

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Provider | Data Collected |
|-------------|---------|----------|-----------------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics unique ID | Google Analytics | Session ID, pageviews |
| `_gat` | Google Analytics throttling | Google Analytics | Request rate |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics session ID | Google Analytics | Session-specific data |

**Google Analytics data:**
- Which pages you visit
- Time spent on pages
- Links clicked
- Referral source (where you came from)
- Browser and device type
- Anonymized IP address

**Privacy:** Your IP address is anonymized (last octate truncated), so individual users can't be identified.

**Opt-out methods:**
1. **Our cookie banner:** Click "Essential Only" when you first visit
2. **Browser settings:** Disable all cookies (note: may break functionality)
3. **Google Opt-out:** https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
4. **Your browser:** Settings > Privacy > Cookies > Block analytics cookies

### 3. Preference Cookies (Optional)

**Status:** Optional - Improve your experience

**Purpose:** Remember your settings and preferences

**Cookies:**

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Expires | Data |
|-------------|---------|---------|------|
| `language_preference` | Remember chosen language | 12 months | Language code (e.g., "es", "en") |
| `theme_preference` | Dark/light mode preference | 12 months | "dark" or "light" |
| `cookieConsent` | Your cookie choices | 12 months | "all" or "essential" |
| `notifications_enabled` | Push notification preference | 12 months | true/false |
| `tutorial_completed` | Whether you saw onboarding | 30 days | true/false |

**Personal info:** These don't contain personal information, just your preferences.

**Opt-out:** Delete via browser settings (won't break anything, just lose your preferences).

### 4. Third-Party Cookies

**Cookies from other companies:**

#### Payment Processing (Stripe, PayPal)

| Provider | Purpose | Cookie | Expires |
|----------|---------|--------|---------|
| Stripe | Process payments securely | `m` | Session |
| PayPal | PayPal payment processing | Various | Session |

**What they track:** Payment-related data only (transaction ID, payment status). Not your browsing history.

**Data handling:** Governed by Stripe and PayPal privacy policies.

#### Authentication (Google, GitHub)

| Provider | Purpose | When Active |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Google OAuth | Sign in with Google | Only if you use this method |
| GitHub OAuth | Sign in with GitHub | Only if you use this method |

**Data:** Only if you explicitly choose to sign in with Google/GitHub.

**What's shared:** Your email address and basic profile (you authorize this during sign-in).

**Governed by:** Google and GitHub privacy policies.

## How to Manage Cookies

### Option 1: Our Cookie Banner

**When you first visit:**
1. A banner appears at the bottom
2. Click **"Accept All"** to allow all cookies
3. Click **"Essential Only"** to refuse analytics/preferences

**To change later:**
- Look for "Cookie Settings" link in footer
- Or delete cookies from browser (see below)

### Option 2: Browser Settings

**Chrome:**
1. Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
2. "Block all cookies" or "Block third-party cookies"
3. Or manage per-site

**Firefox:**
1. Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
2. Adjust settings or delete

**Safari:**
1. Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and Website Data
2. Manage exceptions and delete

**Edge:**
1. Settings > Privacy > Cookies and Site Permissions
2. Manage

### Option 3: Opt-out Tools

**Google Analytics Opt-out:**
https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

**Your Online Choices (EU):**
http://www.youronlinechoices.com/ - Opt out of behavioral advertising

**Network Advertising Initiative (NAI):**
https://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/

### Option 4: Contact Us

Email **support@luxscaler.com** with "Cookie Preferences" and we can help.
